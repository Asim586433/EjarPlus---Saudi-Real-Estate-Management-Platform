About EjarPlus

EjarPlus is Saudi Arabia's leading real estate management platform, designed to streamline property management processes while ensuring full compliance with Saudi regulations.

Our platform connects property managers, real estate owners, tenants, and service providers in a seamless digital ecosystem that simplifies leasing, maintenance, payments, and communication.

Our Mission

To transform the real estate industry in Saudi Arabia through technology innovation, regulatory compliance, and exceptional user experiences for all stakeholders in the property management lifecycle.

Regulatory Compliance

EjarPlus is fully integrated with Saudi government systems including Ejar for lease contract e-signing and Nafath for identity verification, ensuring that all transactions are legally compliant and secure.
